Gen Z Vocabulary

Why Must They Speak in Code?

  1. Every generation develops their own slang based on the conditions in which they are living in.
  2. The addition of technology and internet has greatly influenced how people communicate with each other.
  3. Since texting has become a normal way to communicate with each other, people have developed an easier way to talk to people without having to spell complicated words.

Gen Z Slang

Ate: To do really well, or to impress.
You ate and left no crumbs.
Bet: Another way to say 'yes' or 'absolutely.' Could also mean to challenge.
You think I can't do this? Bet.
Cap: That has to be a lie.
You really liked that movie? That's cap.
Chat: Short for 'groupchat.' Usually said when directing statement at multiple people.
Chat, I really failed that test.
Lowkey/Highkey: Another way to say 'really.'
I lowkey hate this class.
Rizz: Short for 'charisma.'
That dude really has the rizz.
Understanding the assignment: They knew what they were doing.
Wow guys she really understood the assignment.
